## Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor at the [Amsterdam School of Communication Research (ASCoR)](https://ascor.uva.nl/?cb) in the Program Group [Communication, Organizations and Society](http://www.corpcomm.nl/). I currently also serve as project coordinator in the infrastructure project [Twi-XL](https://twi-xl.humanities.uva.nl/).

My research broadly focuses on different organizations’ political (crisis) communication, with an emphasis on the European Union and legitimation processes. In addition, my work also revolves around the societal impact of information and communication technologies, including the use of computational methods.

---

## Research interests

- Political and crisis communication by organizations (especially EU institutions)
- Legitimation processes in European Union politics
- Societal impact of information and communication technologies
- Computational methods and automated text analysis
- Media–politics dynamics during periods of crisis

## Academic career (short overview)

Before joining ASCoR in September 2022, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the [Computational Communication Science Lab (CCL)](https://compcommlab.univie.ac.at/) at the University of Vienna.

During my time at the University of Vienna, I was principal investigator of the research project **“Tango on a Tightrope – An Investigation of Media–Politics Dynamics During Ten Years of EU Crises”**, funded by the Austrian Science Fund’s (FWF) women’s career development scheme (Hertha-Firnberg Program T-989 G27; now substituted by the [ESPRIT program](https://www.fwf.ac.at/en/funding/portfolio/careers/esprit)). I was also Co-PI of an extension project funded by the [Anniversary Fund of the Austrian National Bank](https://www.oenb.at/en/About-Us/Research-Promotion/The-OeNB-Anniversary-Fund.html), which focused on the case study of Austria.

From June 2017 to September 2018, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in the coordinating team of the Horizon2020 project [Transsol – Transnational Solidarity in Times of Crisis](http://www.transsol.eu).

I conducted my pre-doctoral research at the [Institute for Advanced Studies (IHS)](http://www.ihs.ac.at) in Vienna, where I was a member of the **PACE – Parliamentary Communication of Europe** project, financed by the Austrian Science Fund (October 2012 – August 2016).

From September 2020 until February 2021, I was a Virtual Research Fellow at ASCoR, more specifically at the [Center for Politics and Communication](https://www.polcomm.org/).
